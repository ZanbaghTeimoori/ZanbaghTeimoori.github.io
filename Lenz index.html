I am a passionate interior and landscape designer with a strong foundation in design software. I earned my bachelor's degree in interior architecture and my master's degree in landscape architecture, and have since worked on a variety of projects, ranging from country villas to manufacturing companies and apartment renovations. My approach to design is to always put the user first, ensuring that my designs not only meet their needs but that they also exceed their expectations.
